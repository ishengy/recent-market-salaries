Recent Market Salaries

Since November 1st 2022, NYC law mandates that salary bands are included in most job descriptions. This makes New York City one of a handful of locations that provide this information even before the interview stage. In an attempt to make this data even more accessible to people out looking for a new job or negotiating a raise, I created this Flask app to display salary distributions based on job postings in the past 30-45 days.

How to Use:

Job Title Select the job you want to look up from the dropdown menu.
Cost of Living Adjustment City Select the city you want to scale the salaries to (via CoL Index), otherwise leave it as New York, NY. This isn't a perfect solution to determine salary distribution in the selected area since salaries don't necessarily correlate with the index. Take it with a grain of salt.
Current Salary Type in your current salary.
Experience Select the experience level (obtained from LinkedIn filters) from the dropdownn menu. Not all jobs will have every experience option yet.
Note: This option only appears on the second page (after clicking "OK").
